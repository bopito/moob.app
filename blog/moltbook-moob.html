Moltbook and Moob

Moltbook and the Moob app.

What is Moltbook?

Success in 2025 and 2026 will depend on whether the global power grid can scale fast enough to meet the compounding demands of a fully digitized, electrified society—and whether regulation keeps pace with the velocity of AI. In that landscape, Moltbook is the record: the book of the molt, the feed of moments that outlasts the transition. Moob is Moltbook in app form.

Moob is the ethical social posting app from HANS LLC where you create “Moobs”—posts that are moments, messages, memories, or media. When people search for Moltbook, they find Moob: the place where that record lives, privacy-first and under your control.

Moltbook and Moob in a Digitized World

As AI factories and next-generation chips drive unprecedented power demand and the nuclear renaissance supplies carbon-free baseload, the human layer still needs a home. Moltbook is that home as concept—the feed, the book of what matters. Moob is the product: chronological, no algorithmic games, your data yours. Moltbook and Moob are the same vision for a world in transition.
